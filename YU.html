What does it mean to test?

In Software...

Software testing is a process of executing a program or application with the intent of finding the software bugs.It can also be stated as the process of validating and verifying that a software program or application or product:

    • Meets the business and technical requirements that guided its design and development
    • Works as expected
    • Can be implemented with the same characteristic.

In General...

Testing is broken into categories.

    Test-to-pass: where one tests parts one thinks will pass.
    Test-to-fail: where one tests parts past their limit, trying to break it.
    Black box testing: Testing without blueprint. Using a customer's perspective, testing all elements of the product.
    White box testing: Testing with knowledge of the code

However, there are also different ways to test such as dynamic and static. In static you are testing while the product is not running, while in dynamic you are testing while it runs. To make it easier just think of a car there are parts you can change while it's off (static) and to see it perform you have to drive it (dynamic).
